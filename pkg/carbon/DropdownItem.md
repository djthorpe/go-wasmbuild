# DropdownItem

## Constructors

|Constructor|Description|
|----|----|
|`carbon.DropdownItem(args ...any)`|Returns a `cds-dropdown-item` view.|

## State

|Method|Description|
|----|----|
|`Value() string`|Returns the item value.|
|`SetValue(string)`|Sets the item value.|
|`Active() bool`|Returns true when the item is selected.|
|`SetActive(bool)`|Marks the item selected or not selected.|
